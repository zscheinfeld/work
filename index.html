Zach Scheinfeld is a multidisciplinary designer, artist, and developer working towards an MFA in Graphic Design at the Rhode Island School of Design. He is interested in pushing the limitations of web experiences and using the browser as a generative tool to build a more creative, fun, and thoughtful internet.

Zach has worked with design agencies such as Studio Rodrigo and Work & Co in Brooklyn, NY. He is available for select web, branding, or related design projects upon request. You can reach him at zscheinf@gmail.com
